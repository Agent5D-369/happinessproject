Place matters

Why Ojochal is central to the mission

Ojochal is one of the rare Costa Rican communities where local families, entrepreneurs, and an international resident population can genuinely build something together if the right bridge-building infrastructure exists.

Happiness Project exists to be part of that infrastructure through language learning, economic opportunity, social connection, and more intentional support for the local service and founder ecosystem.
