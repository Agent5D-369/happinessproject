Why it fits here

A stronger business and economic-development lane inside Happiness Project

Build Better Together strengthens all three pillars of Happiness Project at once: learning through practical education, economic development through stronger local business support, and social interaction through real collaboration between Ticos and internationals.

It is designed to serve local entrepreneurs first while also helping international residents and founders participate more responsibly in the life of the region.

Business Lab FAQs

Questions founders ask before they reach out

Who is Build Better Together for?

The program is for local entrepreneurs, international founders, land stewards, and community builders who want stronger business foundations and healthier collaboration.

Is this only for international founders?

No. The page is intentionally built for both local businesses and international participants, with bilingual pathways and scholarship-supported local access.

What is the first step if I am not ready for a full program?

The first step is the Regenerative Venture Readiness Scan or the intro seminar, both of which help people see what is breaking down before they commit to a deeper cohort or intensive.
