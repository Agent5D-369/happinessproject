Volunteer FAQs

Questions potential volunteers usually have first

Can I volunteer even if I am not a professional teacher?

Yes. Some roles are ideal for experienced teachers, but others fit mentors, event helpers, language exchange volunteers, translators, and practical community supporters.

Is this only for short-term visitors?

No. The page supports local residents, seasonal visitors, remote contributors, and people exploring a longer-term relationship with the Ojochal community.

Can I volunteer in both English and Spanish?

Yes. Bilingual ability is especially helpful, but there are also roles for English speakers, Spanish speakers, and people still growing into stronger language confidence.
