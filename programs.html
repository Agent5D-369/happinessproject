Program FAQs

Questions people ask before they join

Are there both English and Spanish classes in Ojochal?

Yes. Happiness Project offers English learning pathways for Ticos and Spanish classes for expats and internationals who want stronger daily-life communication and community integration.

Who are the programs designed for?

Programs are designed for local adults, youth, expats, founders, volunteers, and visitors seeking practical language learning, community events, and stronger bridge-building in Costa Rica.

Can I ask about a program before I commit?

Yes. The inquiry flow is meant to route people into the right class, workshop, cohort, or founder pathway before they spend time or money in the wrong lane.
