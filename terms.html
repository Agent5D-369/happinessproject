Website terms

Terms of Use

These terms explain the basic rules for using the Happiness Project Ojochal website, directory, content, and inquiry tools.

Informational use

This website is intended to help people explore programs, events, directory listings, volunteering, visits, and support opportunities.

Changing information

Programs, schedules, listings, availability, and partnership details may change. Visitors should confirm important details before relying on them.

Respectful use

You may not misuse forms, scrape contact details for unrelated marketing, interfere with the site, or submit misleading information.

Directory and community content

How to interpret listings, events, and community information

Directory listings, community resources, and event descriptions are provided to improve connection and visibility. Unless a listing is expressly labeled otherwise, website content should not be read as a guarantee of quality, licensing status, or suitability for your situation.

You remain responsible for checking fit, pricing, timing, credentials, and safety before hiring a provider, attending an event, or entering into an agreement with another party.

Content and intellectual property

Ownership, reuse, and fair use of site content

Unless otherwise stated, site copy, design, structure, and original media are controlled by Happiness Project Ojochal or used with permission.

You may quote or share short excerpts with attribution for non-deceptive purposes, but you should not reproduce or republish substantial portions of the site as if they were your own.

Programs, donations, and partnerships

Important practical boundaries

Submitting a form does not automatically create enrollment, volunteer placement, sponsorship acceptance, or any contractual relationship. Additional agreements, confirmations, or screening steps may be required.

If donations, sponsorships, or paid services are processed through separate tools or agreements, those additional terms will govern the transaction.

Applicable law

Costa Rica context

These terms should be interpreted consistently with the laws of Costa Rica and the local community context in which Happiness Project operates.

This page is a practical website-use baseline, not a substitute for organization-specific legal advice.
