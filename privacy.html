Privacy and data handling

Privacy Policy

This notice explains how Happiness Project Ojochal handles personal information shared through inquiries, program interest, volunteer applications, sponsorship requests, and directory submissions.

Data controller: Happiness Project Ojochal, Corner of Principal and Papagayo, Ojochal de Osa, Puntarenas, Costa Rica.

Scope: This notice applies to information collected through this website and related follow-up communications.

Important: Please do not submit sensitive personal data or medical information through these forms.

What we collect

Personal information tied to specific community requests

Contact details

Name, email, WhatsApp number, language preference, and related details needed to answer you.

Application details

Program interest, volunteer skills, directory submission details, sponsorship interests, and relevant context you choose to share.

Operational records

Basic notes required to route an inquiry, follow up, and maintain accurate records of community participation.

How we use it

Informed, limited use tied to the purpose you chose

We use personal data to respond to inquiries, coordinate visits, evaluate volunteer and directory submissions, discuss sponsorship or partnership requests, and improve how community requests are routed.

Under Costa Rica's Law No. 8968, we aim to collect information in a way that is express, precise, and tied to a stated purpose. If you do not provide the information needed to identify you and respond, we may not be able to process your request.

Rights and retention

Your rights over your data

You may request access to your personal data, ask for corrections, request deletion when appropriate, or object to uses that exceed the purpose for which the data was provided.

We aim to respond to rights requests within the period applicable under Costa Rican law, including the five-business-day response window referenced in Law No. 8968 where it applies.

We keep personal data only as long as it remains useful and necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, and we should avoid retaining outdated or unnecessary information.

Sharing and service providers

How information may be shared

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared with service providers or collaborators only when necessary to operate the website, communicate with you, or process the specific request you made.

Directory listings intended for publication are a special case: only the information meant for public listing should be published, and that publication should be consistent with the purpose explained to the applicant.

This website currently relies on third-party infrastructure such as GitHub Pages and Google Fonts. If additional analytics, advertising, or tracking tools are added later, this notice should be updated and any needed consent flow should be implemented before use.

Questions or requests

How to reach us about privacy

To ask a privacy question or exercise a data right, use the contact page and clearly note that your request relates to personal data or privacy.

Where registration, protocols, or other obligations become applicable under Costa Rica's data-protection framework, they should be maintained through the corresponding PRODHAB process rather than being assumed here without confirmation.
