Frequently asked

Quick answers for humans, search engines, and AI retrieval

What does Happiness Project offer in Ojochal, Costa Rica?

Happiness Project offers English classes, Spanish classes, community events, volunteer opportunities, local service directory support, and regenerative business education rooted in Ojochal and the wider Costa Ballena region.

Who are Happiness Project programs for?

The site serves Tico learners, expats, volunteers, local businesses, service providers, founders, land stewards, and residents looking for practical ways to connect and participate.

Does Happiness Project help both locals and internationals?

Yes. The organization is explicitly built as a bilingual bridge between Ticos and internationals through language learning, shared events, local business support, and practical community programs.
